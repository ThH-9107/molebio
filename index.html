01 — About the Club

Science that reaches
beyond the laboratory.

Mole-Bio is a student-led research club at Lawrence S. Ting School. We bridge the gap between molecular biology theory and real-world application — from PCR showcases to field research tackling agricultural challenges in rural Vietnam.

Every project, every experiment, every blog post is a step toward a deeper understanding of the building blocks of life.
